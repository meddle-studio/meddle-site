Strategic Partnership

Senior creative direction and brand strategy, without the full-time commitment.

You're scaling. Your brand needs consistent oversight, strategic direction, and creative firepower—but you're not ready for (or don't need) a $250K+ full-time executive hire. That's where I come in. Strategic Partnership gives you direct access to a founder-led creative partner with 15 years of design and development leadership—the strategic depth of a creative agency with the agility and accountability of working with one seasoned expert.

What You Get

Think of this as your brand's insurance policy and growth accelerator rolled into one. I become an extension of your team—providing strategic guidance, creative direction, and hands-on support whenever you need it. You get a "mini agency" for the price of one experienced creative, with zero overhead.

Strategic Guidance

  • Monthly strategy sessions (virtual or in-person)
  • Brand positioning and messaging refinement
  • Go-to-market planning for new products or initiatives
  • Competitive landscape analysis and opportunity identification

Creative Direction

  • On-call art direction for campaigns, launches, and key materials
  • Review and feedback on work produced by your internal team or other vendors
  • Creative problem-solving for complex brand challenges
  • Design system governance and brand consistency oversight

Stakeholder Support

  • Executive presentations and board-level communications
  • Client or investor pitch deck review and refinement
  • Internal brand training and team workshops
  • Partner and vendor management (when brand-adjacent)

Ongoing Brand Stewardship

  • Regular brand health check-ins and optimization recommendations
  • Proactive identification of opportunities or threats
  • Access to my network of specialists (developers, copywriters, photographers, etc.)
  • Slack or email access for quick questions and course-correction

How It Works

Strategic Partnership is flexible by design. We establish a monthly commitment (typically 15-30 hours) and a communication cadence that works for your team. Some months you'll use every hour. Other months, you might need just a quick strategy check-in. Either way, you have consistent access to senior-level expertise.

Typical Monthly Rhythm:

  • 1-2 scheduled strategy or planning sessions
  • Ongoing Slack/email access for ad-hoc questions
  • Review of 2-4 key deliverables or initiatives
  • 1 stakeholder presentation or workshop (as needed)

Partnership agreements typically run 3-12 months, with the option to scale up, scale down, or renew based on your evolving needs.

Investment

Strategic Partnership starts at $8K/month for 15 hours of dedicated time and strategic access. Most clients invest $10K-$15K/month depending on scope and intensity. Custom arrangements available for unique situations.

Is Strategic Partnership Right for You?

This offering is built for:

  • Growing companies (Series A-C startups, scaling SMBs) that need executive-level creative talent but aren't ready to hire full-time
  • Organizations with internal design teams who need strategic oversight and elevated creative direction
  • Companies navigating a critical growth phase (fundraising, acquisition, major product launch)
  • Leadership teams who value having a trusted creative advisor on speed-dial
  • Brands that have already worked with Meddle and want to maintain momentum

If you need a complete brand built from scratch, explore Brand Immersion or Brand Sprints. If you're looking for a one-time project, those offerings are better suited.
